HN Job Tracker

Welcome to Hacker News Job Tracker. Hacker News is a social website for posting 'anything that gratifies one's intellectual curiosity'

A regular "Who Is Hiring?" thread appears on the first weekday of each month. Most job ads are welcome there. [FAQ]

This tool ranks job ads by highly advanced natural language processing algorithms searching for buzz words within the text and displays them to the user. The user can then save them for later, apply to them or trash them if they are irrelevant.

Note: This tool does not apply to the jobs for you. You have to apply to them externally, this tool is just a way to keep track of the job posts.
